Bangladesh is a beautiful country in South Asia, known for its rich culture, history, and natural beauty.
It is home to the world's largest river delta, the Sundarbans mangrove forest, and the longest natural sea beach in Cox's Bazar.

The country has a population of over 160 million people, making it one of the most densely populated countries in the world. The people of Bangladesh are known for their hospitality, warmth, and resilience in the face of adversity.
